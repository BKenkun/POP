# **App Name**: PuroRush

## Core Features:

- Product Catalog: Product Catalog: Display available Rush products (Rush Winter 10ml, Gold Rush 10ml, Rush Amarillo 10ml, Rush Butanol 10ml, Rush Zero 10ml, Super Rush 10ml, Super Rush Black Label 10ml) with high-quality images and prices sourced directly from the configuration of the site. Utilize persuasive visuals and sensory language to enhance product appeal and trigger emotional responses, leveraging techniques such as anchoring and framing to influence purchasing decisions.
- Shopping Cart: Shopping Cart: Allow users to easily add and remove products from a cart. Implement visual cues such as progress indicators and dynamic updates to reinforce purchase commitment and reduce cart abandonment. Incorporate scarcity messaging and limited-time offers to drive urgency and encourage immediate action.
- Stripe Integration: Stripe Integration: Integrate Stripe for seamless and secure payment processing, handling transactions directly from the cart without backend processing. Highlight security badges and trust seals to build confidence and alleviate purchase anxiety. Offer multiple payment options to cater to diverse customer preferences and minimize friction.
- Order Summary: Order Summary: Show a clear and concise order summary before redirecting to Stripe for payment. Use a clean and intuitive layout to reinforce the value proposition and minimize cognitive load. Include personalized recommendations and cross-selling opportunities to increase order value and enhance customer satisfaction.
- Checkout Page: Checkout Page: A simple and streamlined checkout page that redirects users to Stripe to complete the transaction. Minimize distractions and streamline the checkout process to reduce friction and increase conversion rates. Provide clear and concise instructions and support options to address any potential concerns or objections.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep indigo (#4B0082) to evoke trust, sophistication, and exclusivity. Use color psychology to create a sense of calm and confidence, encouraging customers to proceed with their purchase.
- Background color: Light lavender (#E6E6FA) to provide a soft, calming backdrop that enhances product visibility and reduces visual fatigue. Create a harmonious and inviting atmosphere that encourages exploration and engagement.
- Accent color: Magenta (#FF00FF) for call-to-action buttons to draw attention and create a sense of excitement and urgency. Use strategically placed accents to guide the user's eye and drive desired actions.
- Headline font: 'Belleza', sans-serif, to convey elegance and modernity. For body text: 'Alegreya', serif, to ensure readability and create a sense of trustworthiness and reliability.
- Simple, modern icons for navigation and product details that communicate information quickly and intuitively. Use visual metaphors and symbols that resonate with the target audience to enhance comprehension and engagement.
- Clean, minimalist layout for easy navigation and a focus on product display. Use whitespace strategically to create a sense of spaciousness and reduce cognitive overload. Implement a clear visual hierarchy to guide the user's eye and highlight key elements.
- Subtle animations for product loading and cart updates to provide visual feedback and enhance the user experience. Use animations to create a sense of dynamism and engagement, reinforcing purchase decisions and driving conversions.
